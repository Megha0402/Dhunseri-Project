About us

Dhunseri
A Century of Trust

learn more

Dhunseri Poly Films Private Limited, a wholly owned subsidiary of Dhunseri Ventures Limited, was incorporated on 28th November 2020. It established Eastern India’s first BOPET film manufacturing plant – a Greenfield Project of 10.5 meter Bruckner polyester film (BOPET) production line in Panagarh, West Bengal, which commenced production in December 2023.

The company is focused on producing high-quality BOPET films to serve various industries with a vision to achieve worldwide eminence through quality leadership and adherence to best global practices. We consider Sustainable Production, Best Quality Control and Worldwide Shipping as our three driving factors towards excellence.

Dhunseri Group

A Century of Trust

The Dhunseri Group is one of the oldest business houses in India, headquartered in Kolkata, flourishing under the leadership of Mr. Chandra Kumar Dhanuka. The group has interests in multiple sectors, including PET chips production (with plants in India and Egypt in JV with the Indorama Group of Thailand), Tea plantations in Assam (India) and Malawi (Africa), Confectionery retail business in Singapore with a recent expansion into the U.S. market. The group has been able to achieve success in its endeavours using three vital tools – Foresight, Planned strategies and Preparedness to deal with the present and future of our business.
